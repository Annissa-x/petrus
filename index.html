"You can't control what happens to you, only how you respond."

Elena Vasquez · Stoicism Teacher · Austin, TX

I teach practical Stoicism — the daily exercises, the journaling practices, the mental frameworks that Marcus Aurelius and Seneca used to stay sane in chaotic times. This isn't about suppressing emotions. It's about not being at the mercy of things you can't control. Most people come to me overwhelmed by work, comparison, or a sense that they're falling behind. We work on that.
